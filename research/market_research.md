# Market Research: AI Cover Letter

## Market Overview

The job search automation market is experiencing significant growth, driven by:
- Increased competition in the job market post-pandemic
- Rise of AI-powered productivity tools
- Remote work opportunities expanding globally

## Target Audience

**Primary Users:**
- Job seekers aged 22-45
- Professionals looking to switch careers
- Recent graduates entering the job market
- Mid-career professionals seeking advancement

**User Pain Points:**
- Writing personalized cover letters for each application
- ATS (Applicant Tracking System) keyword optimization
- Time-consuming application process
- Lack of confidence in writing skills

## Market Size

- Total addressable market (TAM): $4.2B (job search tools)
- Serviceable addressable market (SAM): $850M (AI writing assistants)
- Serviceable obtainable market (SOM): $8.5M (Year 1 target)

## Trends

1. **AI Adoption**: 73% of employers use AI for initial screening
2. **Mobile Job Search**: 67% of job searches happen on mobile
3. **Personalization**: Customized applications have 40% higher response rates
4. **Speed**: Fast application submission increases success by 25%

## Opportunities

- Integration with LinkedIn for profile data
- ATS compatibility checking
- Industry-specific templates
- Multi-language support
