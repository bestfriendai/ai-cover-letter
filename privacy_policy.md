# Privacy Policy: AI Cover Letter

**Last Updated:** February 2026

## Data Collection

AI Cover Letter collects minimal data to provide its services:

### Data We Collect

- **Resume Text**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Generated Letters**: Saved to your personal library (local storage)
- **App Usage**: Anonymous analytics for improvement

### Data We DON'T Collect

- We do not upload your resume or cover letters to any server
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not sell your personal information

## Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using:
- AsyncStorage for preferences
- SQLite for saved letters

You can delete all data at any time from Settings.

## Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat for subscription management. They collect:
- Purchase history
- Device identifiers
- Subscription status

Their privacy policy applies: [revenuecat.com/privacy](https://revenuecat.com/privacy)

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify users of material changes.
